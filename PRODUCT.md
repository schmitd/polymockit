# Polymockit Product Context

Polymockit is a fantasy prediction-market league app for groups that want the texture of Polymarket without real-money stakes. Members join private leagues, receive a fake bankroll, browse live Polymarket markets, buy positions, and compare marked-to-market standings.

## Users

- League commissioners who create leagues, invite friends, and keep the game moving.
- League members who scan markets, place positions, follow leaderboards, and cash out.
- Curious prediction-market users who need enough market detail to act without being buried in exchange-grade tooling.

## Product Purpose

Make fantasy market trading feel immediate, legible, and competitive. The interface should support fast scanning, confident action, and a clear distinction between league state, market state, and trade entry.

## Tone

Focused, crisp, and game-day operational. The app should feel like a compact commissioner desk, not a casino, crypto terminal, or marketing page.

## Strategic Principles

- Keep the task surface dense but organized. Users should compare markets, standings, and positions without opening extra views.
- Prioritize confidence over decoration. Every color, panel, and line should clarify state or hierarchy.
- Make risky actions explicit. Buying and cashing out should have strong affordances, disabled states, and clear labels.
- Keep league identity visible. Invite code, standings, cash, and recent bets are core context, not secondary metadata.

## Anti-References

- Neon crypto dashboards, glowing gradients, glass panels, and decorative blur.
- Generic SaaS hero metrics.
- Soft beige editorial dashboards.
- Overly playful game UI that makes trading actions feel unserious.
